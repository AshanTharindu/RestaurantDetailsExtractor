Restaurants (KAMU)

This is our guide to restaurants in Colombo and Sri Lanka.
  1. Place Types
  2. Restaurants (KAMU)

New Places

Epicure

65/B R G Senanayake Mawatha, Colombo 00700

Epicure has great service and ambience, well in line with their market bracket. But the food falls short of what you would ideally expect. Still, it´s early days for this restaurant and we´re looking forward…

Pastamania

502 R A De Mal Mawatha, Colombo 3

A Singapore based Italian franchise opens its first outlet in Sri Lanka

The Biryani Restaurant

33 Armour Street, Colombo 12

Authentic Indian Biriyani for an affordable price.

Sugar Beach

Mount Lavinia Beach, Mount Lavinia, Sri Lanka

The newest spot on Mount Beach has food that is more hit than miss, plus open spaces with new agey vibes that spill onto the beach.

La Veranda

55, Ananda Coomarasamy Mawatha, Colombo 03

La Veranda is one of the newest cafes in town. Their food is alright, but the place itself needs a lot of improvement.

Cafe Kai

Hilton Hotel, Colombo 1

As far as hotel lobbies go, Kafe Kai provides a great vibe for work meetings, for anything else it's a tad too impersonal for our taste

Isle of Gelato

60A Pedlar Street, Galle

Isle of Gelato serves some serious killer gelato. You should try.

Calorie Counter Ward Place

59, Ward Place, Colombo 07

The new Calorie Counter in Ward Place has better parking facilities than its compatriot in Thimbirigasyaya, but that's pretty much the best thing I can say about the place.

Weligama Kitchen (Marriot Weligama)

700 Matara Road, Pelana, Weligama

The Weligama Kitchen is the main restaurant at the Marriot Weligama, it serves an expansive range of local and foreign cuisines that rival the price and quality of similar establishments.

Big Fish (Marriot Weligama)

700 Matara Road, Pelana, Weligama

Big Fish provides a sea food fine dining experience that can probably satisfy the biggest food pedant at a cost that, while not cheap , certainly delivers value for money.

Tides (Marriot Weligama)

700 Matara Road, Pelana, Weligama

A luxurious bar/lounge/dinner set up that doesn’t cost as much as it feels. The Discovery Table concept offers a value for money experience.

Wheelys Café

100, Jawatte Road, Colombo 5

After cycling around Colombo with their solar powered cart for about a year, Wheelys has settled down in on Jawatte Road. In addition to their single origin fair-trade coffee, they now also serve…

Wing Spot

123 & 127 Vinayalankara Mawatha Colombo, Sri Lanka

A new place nestled at The Food Station specially dedicated to making chicken wing platters. Whether its an answer to people's prayers or not, this place does have an intriguing variety of sauces for you…

Takeout Lanka

No.116, Union Place, Colombo 02

Bangladesh's favourite Burger joint is now available in Sri Lanka. The burgers are tasty and all, but the heavy price tag is questionable.

Botanik

7 Hospital Street, Colombo 1

A very modern bistro/bar on top of the Fairway Hotel.

Cakes & Bakes Café

Bamabalapitya Colombo, Sri Lanka

A premium patisserie with a range of super interesting desserts and short-eats.

The Gourmet Station (Kingsbury Serviced Apartments)

No 1, Bagatalle Road, Colombo 3, Sri Lanka - Galle Main Road, Colombo 03

The Gourmet Station at the Kingsbury Serviced Apartments is a two-by-four snack bar that serves sweet and savoury nibbles for those who want to grab a bite before they go.

Vasantha Bhavan

19/1, Horton Place, Colombo.

Vasantha Bhavan is a pure vegetarian restaurant down Horton Place.

The Chaat Kade

78B, W.A Silva Mawatha, Colombo 06.

An Indian streetfood joint with ridiculously reasonable prices.

JAM Rolled Ice Cream

65, Thimbirigasyaya Road, Colombo 5

So there's a new contender for ice cream in town. Not only is it a visually exciting experience it's also done really well

VU Restobar (Best Western Hotel)

102 A, Kirulapone Avenue, Baseline Road, Colombo 5

VU Restobar is a wonderful rooftop bar at the Best Western Hotel. They serve up some great food, their drinks aren't too shabby and the view is breathtaking.

Street Burgers

Marine Drive, Bambalapitiya

Self explanatory, they serve up pretty great beef patties and okay grilled chicken with their burgers.

Gill's Food Arcade (First Look)

No. 07 Hostpital Street, Colombo 1

The Fairway Hotel next to Dutch Hospital has a foodcourt.

Sizzlers by Barracuda (First Look)

No. 41/2 Wasala Road, Dehiwela

Sizzlers by Barracuda is a meat-focused resto adjoining Barracuda.

All Places

    We Ai Family

    Union Place, Colombo 2.

    An authentic Chinese restaurant serving huge portions of rice and noodles.

    Wayne's Junction

    No. 304/4, Hokandara Road, Thalawathugoda.

    A cafe that seems to have Australian inspired dishes, as well as local delicacies. Only on paper, as it turned out.

    Salwa Turkish Food

    1B, Albert Place, Dehiwala

    A tiny food joint down Albert Place, Dehiwala that serves up some good street food for very cheap prices.

    Navro Beach Restaurant

    No: 40, Beach road, Panadura

    Navro Beach is one of the most popular restaurants in Panadura area, if you want to have a relaxed evening with some drinks and delicious food.

    Hasara Kitchen

    No: 195 Pagoda Rd, Nugegoda

    Hasara Kitchen is a popular spot in Nugegoda, that dishes out some okay rice and curry for a decent price tag.

    Havi Restaurant

    No:186, B47, Pannipitiya

    Havi at Pannipitiya is a good spot to chill with some booze, and delicious black pepper pork.

    Tea Blog

    758A, Dr. Danister de Silva Mawatha, Colombo 09

    The newest bubble tea joint in Colombo. They also do cakes, burgers, waffles and sandwiches.

    The Woods

    Sembuwatte state, Nikapotha, Haputale

    A beautiful cottage hotel that provides the genuine jungle experience nestled in the wilderness of Beragala.

    Chinese Delight

    41-A, Ridgeway place, Marine drive, Colombo 04

    Chinese Delight is one of Colombo's many, many Chinese eateries. They've got great Chinese food for an affordable price tag, with the nicest service.

    The Regent Cafe

    No. 171, Kynsey Road, Borella, Colombo 8.

    A greatly underrated place serving some of the best lamprais we have had. Highly recommend you check it out!

    Brew 1867 by Dilmah

    No. 38/46 Navam Mawatha, Colombo 2.

    Your ideal place to grab a quick breakfast or lunch, which is both healthy and freshly made.

    The Last Drop

    No. 37, Yatinuwara Street, Kandy.

    A restaurant and pub that serves some excellent dishes.

    The Bungalow - Galle Fort

    No. 5, Church Cross Street, Galle Fort, Galle

    With its colonial elegance, comfortable accommodation, well trained staff, and the excellent food, The Bungalow - Galle Fort is a place that unites every bit of happiness that you're looking for in Galle.…

    Podi Hoona - The Little Gecko Luxury Campsite

    Yala National Park, Kirinda - Sithulpawwa road, Thissamaharamaya

    An incredibly beautiful luxury campsite which is a treat for eco-lovers, birdwatchers, and people who enjoy genuine rusticity.

    Awanhala (Ambepussa)

    Kandy Road, Ambepussa.

    A pitstop to refresh yourself with a fine cup of tea on your way to Kandy.

    Breeze Beverages & Bakery

    No. 198, D.S Senanayake Veediya, Kandy 2.

    A small gem in the busy Kandy streets serving some great coffee and deliciously sweet treats.

    Sahan Agro Strawberry Corner

    Jambugasmulla Mawatha, Nugegoda

    The Sahan Agro organic shop at Economic Center has a dessert parlour down Jambugasmulla Mawatha, Nugegoda. They dishes out a range of strawberry based snacks, drinks and other natural products like bee honey.…

    Sea Breeze Seafood Restaurant

    Hiriketiya Road, Dickwella.

    Great simple Sri Lankan and fusion/international food on Hiriketiya beach.

    Beach House

    Hiriketiya Beach, Dikwella.

    Great pizza place in Hiri with rather slow service.

    Bara Beach Home

    97, Galle Road, Pitiwella 80280 Galle

    bed and breakfast with cabanas on a relatively isolated patch of beach

    Tokiwa Cafe

    No 213, Galle Road, Colombo 3.

    Dwell Sri Lanka's Japanese inspired cafe where you can enjoy great food within a relaxed space.

    Pages & Coffee

    No. 437 /1, Cotta Road, Rajagiriya.

    A great place to sit and enjoy some organic coffee amongst loads of books.

    Red Orchids

    418/1, Pelawatta

    Great please to have a delicious Chinese, Thai dinner freely

    Cafe Sociale

    No. 252A, Galle Road, Colombo 4.

    A retro themed cafe serving filling dishes and average coffee.
